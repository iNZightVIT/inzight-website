## Paste from Clipboard {#paste-from}

You can import data directly from Excel or Google Sheets by selecting the data and copying it (CTRL + C) and then using the **Paste from...** option in the **File** menu to paste the data directly into iNZight.

Click **Load** when finished.

## Copy to Clipboard {#copy-to}

You can also copy your current dataset to the clipboard to paste into other applications:

1. Go to **File** → **Clipboard** → **Copy to clipboard**
2. Your data is now on the clipboard
3. Paste (CTRL + V) into Excel, Google Sheets, or other applications
